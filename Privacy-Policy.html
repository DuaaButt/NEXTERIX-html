Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

At Nexterix, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your data when you use our services, particularly for business verification and TCR registration processes.

Business Verification

Our website serves as a platform for business verification as part of the TCR registration process. The integrity and functionality of our website are essential to comply with the latest TCR registration requirements.

We maintain strict protocols to ensure that the verification process is secure, transparent, and compliant with all applicable regulations.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information necessary for business verification and TCR registration. This may include, but is not limited to, your full name, contact details, and business information. We use this information exclusively for verification purposes and to fulfill regulatory requirements.

Information we may collect:

  • Full name and job title
  • Business email address and phone numbers
  • Company name, address, and registration details
  • Business identification numbers and documentation
  • Account credentials for our verification services

Opt-In Consent

By submitting your information on our website, you consent to us using your personal details for business verification related to TCR registration. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us.

You may revoke your consent at any time by sending an email to info@nexterix.com with the subject line "Consent Withdrawal" or by using the contact form on our website.

Non-Disclosure to Third Parties

We are committed to ensuring that your information is not shared with third parties and consent with third parties/affiliates or partners. No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.

All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Our Promise to You:

We will never sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented stringent measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Safeguards

  • • SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • • Regular security assessments
  • • Advanced firewall protection

Organizational Measures

  • • Strict access controls
  • • Staff privacy training
  • • Data handling protocols
  • • Regular compliance audits

Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to correct any inaccuracies, or to request the deletion of your data. For any such requests, please contact us using the information provided on our website.

Access

You can request a copy of your personal data that we process.

Correction

You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
