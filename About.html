Our Story

Founded in 2020, NEXTERIX began with a simple mission: to provide businesses with the technology and services they need to thrive in an increasingly digital world. What started as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into a global organization serving clients across multiple industries.

Our journey has been defined by innovation, adaptability, and an unwavering commitment to client success. As technology evolved, so did we – continuously expanding our service offerings to meet the changing needs of our clients.

Today, NEXTERIX stands as a leader in Business Process Outsourcing, Logistics Technology, and Digital Solutions, helping companies of all sizes transform their operations and achieve sustainable growth.
